Malteo Winery

Malteo Winery’s white wines are crafted with meticulous care, blending tradition and innovation to deliver a crisp, refreshing taste that captures the essence of their terroir. Known for their vibrant aromas, balanced acidity, and smooth finish, these wines pair beautifully with a variety of dishes, from light seafood to creamy cheeses. Each sip offers a journey into the heart of Malteo’s vineyards, embodying quality and elegance.

At Malteo Winery, our winemaking process is a blend of tradition and innovation, crafted to bring out the unique character of every grape. From hand-selecting the finest fruit at peak ripeness to carefully controlling fermentation and aging, we focus on quality at every step. Our expert team combines time-honored techniques with modern technology, ensuring each bottle reflects the rich flavors and aromas that define our terroir. Whether it’s our robust reds or crisp whites, every sip tells a story of dedication, passion, and craftsmanship.

Our History

Founded in 1952, Malteo Winery has been a cherished family tradition for over three generations. Nestled in one of New Zealand’s oldest and most renowned wine regions, our vineyards benefit from a unique terroir that imparts exceptional character to our wines. From the very beginning, we have committed to sustainable farming practices and meticulous craftsmanship, blending time-honored techniques with modern innovation. This dedication has allowed us to create elegant, award-winning white wines that capture the true essence of our land and heritage.

  • Malteo Winery has been proudly family-owned and operated since 1952, passing down winemaking traditions and passion from generation to generation.
  • All grapes are cultivated using environmentally friendly and sustainable farming practices, ensuring the health of the land and the quality of every bottle.
  • Malteo Winery’s white wines have received multiple regional and international awards for their crisp, balanced flavors and elegant finishes.
  • The winery’s vineyards are located in one of New Zealand’s oldest wine-growing regions, known for its unique terroir that imparts distinctive character to the wines.

At Malteo Winery, we take pride in our rich heritage and commitment to quality. Our grapes are sustainably grown in pristine vineyards, reflecting our respect for the environment and future generations. Over the years, our wines have garnered multiple awards, showcasing the exceptional care and expertise poured into every bottle. As a family-owned business spanning three generations, we cherish the deep connections we have built with our community and the timeless traditions that inspire each vintage.
